How conditions work

A condition is an item needed before the loan can move forward.
Some conditions are document-based, others may need clarification or updates.
Once reviewed and accepted, the condition can be marked cleared.

Best practices

  • Upload the clearest and most current version of each file.
  • Name files clearly before uploading.
  • Check your messages for follow-up questions from the team.
  • Review your conditions often so nothing is missed.

Need help?
