ONE-PAGER

Ravlo Lending OS

A single operating system for private and non-QM lending teams — borrower intake, pipeline visibility, conditions and compliance, team coordination, and closing execution, with Ravlo AI built into every role's dashboard instead of bolted on as a chatbot.

The problem

Lending teams run on a patchwork of spreadsheets, email threads, and a CRM never built for loan execution. Nobody has one place to see the whole pipeline, and every handoff between loan officer, processor, and underwriter is a manual re-explanation.

The pitch

Every role — loan officer, processor, underwriter, investor, borrower, admin — gets its own dashboard scoped to exactly their work, and Ravlo AI reads that role's live data to summarize what needs attention, instead of the team writing status updates by hand.

Pricing

$149/mo — single seat, for a solo originator or broker.
$799/mo — full team (up to 10 users), for brokerages coordinating loan officers, processors, and admins together.

30-second version

"Ravlo Lending OS replaces your spreadsheets and email chains with one system where every role — loan officer, processor, underwriter, investor, borrower — has their own dashboard and their own AI summary of what's on their plate. It's built for private and non-QM lending specifically, not a generic CRM, and it's $149 a month for a single seat or $799 for a full team of up to 10."

TALKING POINTS

What to say about each Lending OS dashboard

Use these when a company wants to know what their team actually gets, role by role. Lead with the "say this" line, then use the detail underneath if they want more.

LOAN OFFICER

Pipeline Dashboard

"This is where your originators live all day — every deal, sorted by stage, with commission math done for them."

Full pipeline view with borrower conversion tracking, a running commission ledger, and a Ravlo AI pipeline summary that flags which deals are stalling before the loan officer has to go looking for them.

PROCESSOR

Processor Dashboard

"Processors get a single queue of every capital application that needs their eyes, with documents and status in one place."

Combined view of assigned loans plus the company-wide capital submission queue, pending document counts, and a Ravlo AI summary of the day's processing workload.

UNDERWRITER

Underwriter Dashboard

"Underwriters see risk and conditions in one queue, and Ravlo automatically generates compliant adverse action notices when a loan is declined."

Conditions management, risk reports, per-underwriter assignment so nothing gets double-worked, and automatic ECOA adverse action notice generation baked into the decision flow — one less compliance headache for the company.

INVESTOR

Investor Command Center

"Investors get a portfolio snapshot with AI commentary, plus the Deal, Design, and Build Studios to actually plan renovations against a real budget."

Portfolio-wide AI summary, Deal Architect for underwriting new opportunities, and Design/Build Studio tools that generate renovation concepts tied to the deal's real rehab budget — not a generic AI image generator.

BORROWER

Borrower Portal

"Borrowers get a portal with an AI assistant that can explain their own loan status in plain English, 24/7, without tying up a loan officer's time."

Live status tracking, document upload, and a Ravlo AI assistant scoped to that borrower's own loan — it answers "where's my loan at" questions so the loan officer doesn't have to.

ADMIN / OWNER

Company Dashboard

"The company admin sees everything — team, licensing status, billing, and leads — in one control panel, not five different tools."

Team management and invites, per-state loan officer license verification, billing and plan management, and a built-in CRM/lead pipeline (including Facebook/Instagram lead capture) — all scoped to that one company's data only.
