Best Practices

Loan Officer Standards

Move files daily

No active file should sit without action, follow-up, or notes.

Keep communication clear

Borrowers should always know current status and next step.

Document everything

Every call, request, and status change should be reflected in the system.

Escalate blockers early

Flag missing docs, slow responses, or underwriting issues immediately.
