Investor education

Topics that matter in real estate investing.

Understanding ARV and Rehab Costs
After Repair Value sets the ceiling. Knowing how to estimate it accurately — and how rehab costs stack against it — is the foundation of a sound deal analysis.
Fix and Flip vs. Hold Strategy
Each exit strategy carries different timelines, risk profiles, and capital requirements. Ravlo helps you model and compare your options before you commit.
New Construction Basics
Build projects require a different mindset than rehabs. Learn the key variables — land, entitlement, construction cost, and absorption — that determine whether a build pencils.
Working with Partners and Lenders
Most investors scale through relationships. Ravlo's partner ecosystem is built to support that — from contractors to capital partners.
