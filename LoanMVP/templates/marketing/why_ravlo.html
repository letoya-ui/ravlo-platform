The problem

The real estate workflow is fragmented.

Investors and professionals are forced to make important decisions across too many disconnected tools: spreadsheets, CRMs, deal calculators, project trackers, lender portals, AI chats, emails, documents, and notes. That fragmentation creates friction, slows decisions, and makes it harder to see the full picture.

Deals are scattered

Properties, notes, numbers, and comparisons are often spread across tabs, screenshots, and spreadsheets.

Budgets get messy

Projected costs, actual spend, and construction decisions are hard to manage when they live outside the deal.

Capital is disconnected

Funding and refinance conversations often happen separately from the investment plan they are meant to support.

Teams are disconnected

Agents, lenders, contractors, builders, and partners need one connected place to support the opportunity.

The Ravlo answer
