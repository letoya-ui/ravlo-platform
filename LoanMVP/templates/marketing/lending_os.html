What it does

A connected operating system for the lending side of the deal.

Lending OS helps teams centralize the workflow between borrower engagement, internal review, document handling, conditions tracking, and operational follow-through.

Borrower Intake

Capture borrower details, project information, financial data, and supporting documents through a cleaner front-end experience.

Pipeline Visibility

Track loan movement across stages with internal dashboards designed for lending teams that need clarity and speed.

Conditions & Review

Manage underwriting conditions, next steps, and team handoff points in a more organized and consistent environment.

Role-Based Workflows

Support loan officers, processors, underwriters, and operational leadership with role-aware visibility and task flow.

Communication Support

Keep teams aligned with clearer borrower communication, internal updates, and centralized process flow.

AI-Ready Foundation

Built to support intelligent guidance, workflow support, and future automation across the lending lifecycle.
