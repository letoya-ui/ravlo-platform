Why It Works

No cash out of pocket.
Not a dollar.

Most sellers have to hire cleaners, landscapers, painters, and repair crews separately — paying each one upfront, weeks before the sale ever closes. That's thousands out of pocket before you've made a cent.

With my packages, every service is written into the listing contract from day one. My crew does the work. Your home goes to market move-in ready. And everything gets settled at closing from the proceeds — just like the commission always has.

  • 📋
    In writing before we start. Every service is itemized in your listing agreement — scope, timeline, and cost. No verbal promises, no surprises at closing.
  • 💰
    Paid from proceeds, not your pocket. The commission that covers all services is collected at closing, the same way every real estate commission works.
  • 🏡
    Move-in ready listings sell for more. Buyers compete harder for homes that need nothing. Fewer inspection concessions means you keep more of the sale price.
  • 📞
    One call handles everything. Listing, work crews, showings, offers, negotiation, closing — you deal with one person from start to finish.
