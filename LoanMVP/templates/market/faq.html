Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to the most common questions from investors, builders, borrowers, and partners.

General

Understanding Ravlo

What is Ravlo?

Ravlo is an investor operating system — a unified environment where deals are analyzed, planned, funded, and executed with clarity and discipline.

Who is Ravlo for?

Investors, builders, lenders, partners, and operators who want a structured, professional workflow for real estate execution.

Is Ravlo free to use?

Ravlo offers both free and premium plans depending on your workflow needs. Visit the Plans page for details.

Investor Questions

Investors & Deal Analysis

What is the AI Deal Architect?

It’s an AI-powered analysis engine that evaluates flips, rentals, BRRRR, and development opportunities with clean, investor-grade intelligence.

Can I save and compare deals?

Yes — the Deal Pipeline and Compare tools allow you to store, organize, and evaluate opportunities side-by-side.

Does Ravlo help with construction planning?

Yes. Build Studio and Renovation Studio help you plan new construction, visualize renovations, and prepare scopes before breaking ground.

Capital

Borrowers & Funding

How do capital requests work?

Borrowers submit a capital request through Capital Studio, upload required documents, and track underwriting progress in real time.

Does Ravlo provide funding?

Ravlo connects borrowers with lenders and capital partners inside a structured workflow. Ravlo itself does not issue loans.

How long does underwriting take?

Timelines vary by lender, but most reviews begin within 24–48 hours of submission.

Partners

Contractors, Agents & Service Providers

How do partners join Ravlo?

Contractors, agents, lenders, and operators can create a partner profile through the Partners page.

How do partners get matched with projects?

Partners become discoverable inside the ecosystem and can receive invitations from investors and builders based on project needs.

Is there a cost to join the Partner Network?

Basic partner profiles are free. Premium placement and advanced tools may be available in future updates.
