Privacy Policy

Ravlo is built on clarity, discipline, and trust. This policy explains how we handle your information with intention and respect.

Our Approach

Your privacy matters.

Ravlo collects only the information necessary to operate the platform, improve the experience, and support your workflow. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for advertising. We treat your information with the same discipline we bring to the platform itself.

Information We Collect

What we gather — and why.

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email, and basic profile details. This allows you to access the platform and receive essential updates.

Usage Data

We track how you use Ravlo — pages visited, tools accessed, and actions taken. This helps us improve performance, stability, and user experience.

Deal & Project Data

Any information you enter into Deal Architect, Build Studio, Renovation Studio, or Capital Studio is stored securely and used only to power your workflow.

Device & Technical Data

We collect IP addresses, browser type, and system logs to maintain security, prevent abuse, and ensure platform reliability.

How We Use Your Information

Purpose-driven, never excessive.

  • To operate and maintain the Ravlo platform
  • To improve performance, stability, and user experience
  • To provide support and respond to inquiries
  • To secure accounts and prevent unauthorized access
  • To communicate updates, releases, and essential notices
Sharing

Who we share data with.

Ravlo does not sell personal information. We only share data with trusted service providers who help us operate the platform — such as hosting, analytics, and security partners. These providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the services they deliver.

Security

Protecting your information.

We use industry-standard security practices to safeguard your data, including encryption, access controls, and continuous monitoring. No system is perfect, but we take security seriously and improve continuously.

Your Choices

Control over your data.
