Quick-Start Checklist

Complete these five steps to get fully set up.

1
Complete your investor profile

Add your strategy, target markets, and investment criteria so Ravlo can tailor recommendations to you.
2
Find and save your first deal

Search a market in Deal Finder and save at least one property to start your pipeline.
3
Run underwriting in Deal Workspace

Open your saved property in Deal Workspace and let the AI analyze it — strategy, score, and projected returns.
4
Generate a build package

Open Build Studio, describe your project, and generate AI blueprints and exterior renderings. Budget costs auto-populate.
5
Submit to Capital Studio

When your deal package is ready, apply for funding through Capital Studio and track every step to close.
6
Build your Partner Network

Connect with contractors, inspectors, and lenders in your market to move faster on every deal.

Pro tip: The fastest path is Deal Finder → Deal Workspace → Build Studio. Save a property, run the AI analysis, generate a build package, and Ravlo automatically prices every line item and lands you in Budget Studio — ready to present to lenders.
