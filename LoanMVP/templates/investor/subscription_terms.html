SUBSCRIPTION AUTHORIZATION

Paid plans

By selecting a paid plan and completing checkout, you authorize Ravlo and its payment processor to charge the payment method you provide for the subscription price, applicable taxes, and any approved plan changes. Subscriptions renew until canceled, downgraded, or otherwise changed from the subscription or billing screen.

CARD DATA PROTECTION

What Ravlo stores

Ravlo uses a third-party payment processor for card entry and recurring billing. Ravlo does not store full card numbers, CVV codes, magnetic stripe data, or chip data on its servers.

Ravlo may receive and retain limited billing records needed to run the account, including card brand, last four digits, expiration month/year, billing contact, receipts, subscription status, Stripe customer or subscription IDs, invoices, charge status, fraud signals, tax records, and support notes.

CONSUMER INFORMATION

How billing data is used

  • To process payments, renewals, refunds, receipts, taxes, and account support.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, unauthorized transactions, and payment disputes.
  • To comply with legal, accounting, tax, banking, and card-network obligations.
  • To limit access to billing records to people and service providers with a business need.

Ravlo does not sell payment card information. Report suspected unauthorized charges or billing security concerns through support so the account and processor records can be reviewed.
