\# Ecosystem Overview



The Ravlo Ecosystem is a circular, architectural map that visualizes how every module, workflow, partner, and intelligence layer connects. It is the master blueprint of the Ravlo universe.



\## Purpose



The Ecosystem Overview answers:

“What is Ravlo, and how does everything work together?”



It provides:

\- a unified mental model  

\- a visual representation of the OS  

\- clarity for investors, partners, and internal teams  

\- a scalable structure for future modules  



\## Structure



The ecosystem is composed of \*\*three concentric rings\*\*:



1\. \*\*Core OS (Center Cube)\*\*  

2\. \*\*Primary Modules (Inner Ring)\*\*  

3\. \*\*Ecosystem Extensions (Outer Ring)\*\*  



Each ring represents a layer of capability, depth, and interaction.



\---



\# 1. Core OS (Center Cube)



The Core OS is the heart of Ravlo — the system that powers everything.



\### Includes:

\- Authentication  

\- User roles  

\- Data engine  

\- AI engine  

\- Notification system  

\- Document engine  

\- System patterns  

\- Visual OS  



\### Purpose:

To provide the foundational infrastructure that all modules rely on.



\---



\# 2. Primary Modules (Inner Ring)



These are the six core modules that investors use to operate their entire real estate workflow.



\### Modules:

\- \*\*Command Center\*\*  

\- \*\*Deal Architect\*\*  

\- \*\*Build Studio\*\*  

\- \*\*Rehab Studio\*\*  

\- \*\*Capital Studio\*\*  

\- \*\*Portfolio Intelligence\*\*  



\### Purpose:

To give investors a complete, end‑to‑end operating system for deals, capital, and portfolio management.



\### Behavior:

\- Each module is independent but interconnected.  

\- Data flows between modules automatically.  

\- AI insights are shared across modules.  



\---



\# 3. Ecosystem Extensions (Outer Ring)



These modules expand Ravlo beyond the investor into a multi‑sided marketplace.



\### Includes:

\- \*\*Partner Marketplace\*\*  

\- \*\*Partner Dashboards\*\*  

\- \*\*Service Requests\*\*  

\- \*\*Lender Network\*\*  

\- \*\*Contractor Network\*\*  

\- \*\*Realtor Network\*\*  

\- \*\*Appraiser / Inspector Network\*\*  



\### Purpose:

To connect investors with the professionals they need to execute deals.



\### Behavior:

\- Partners plug into investor workflows.  

\- Service requests flow into partner dashboards.  

\- AI matches investors with the best partners.  



\---



\# Node Connections



Each module is represented as a \*\*node\*\* connected by architectural blueprint lines.



\### Node Types:

\- \*\*Primary nodes\*\* (modules)  

\- \*\*Secondary nodes\*\* (subsystems)  

\- \*\*Tertiary nodes\*\* (micro‑services or partner roles)  



\### Connection Rules:

\- Lines must be clean, geometric, and evenly spaced.  

\- No chaotic or organic shapes.  

\- All lines originate from the Core OS.
