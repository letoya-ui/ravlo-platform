\# Ravlo Color Palette



\## Core Colors

\- Midnight: #0C1116

\- Steel: #6B7F93

\- Blueprint Blue: #3A5C7A

\- Soft Glow Blue: #4C6E8C

\- White: #FFFFFF



\## Usage Notes

\- Midnight is the primary background.

\- Steel is the base for matte elements.

\- Blueprint Blue is used for accents and subtle glow.

\- Soft Glow Blue is used for OS highlights.
