Motion Tokens



Motion tokens define Ravlo’s timing, easing, and cinematic transitions.



1\. Durations

motion.fast: 120ms

motion.normal: 180ms

motion.slow: 240ms

motion.cinematic: 320ms



2\. Easing

ease.standard: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 0.2, 1)

ease.decelerate: cubic-bezier(0.0, 0.0, 0.2, 1)

ease.accelerate: cubic-bezier(0.4, 0.0, 1, 1)



3\. Motion Types

motion.fade: opacity transition

motion.slide: transform + opacity

motion.scale: subtle scale + fade



Motion tokens create Ravlo’s calm, cinematic movement.
