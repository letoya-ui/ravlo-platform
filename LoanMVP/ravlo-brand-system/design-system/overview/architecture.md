System Architecture



The Ravlo Design System is built like a real architectural framework — layered, modular, and scalable.



1\. Tokens — The Foundation

Tokens define the rules of the world:

\- color

\- spacing

\- typography

\- radii

\- shadows

\- z-index



They are the atomic layer — the physics of Ravlo.



2\. Components — The Building Blocks

Components are the structural elements:

\- buttons

\- cards

\- panels

\- metrics

\- navigation

\- inputs



They are reusable, consistent, and token-driven.



3\. Layouts — The Floorplans

Layouts define the macro-structure:

\- dashboards

\- OS modules

\- marketing pages

\- auth flows

\- content pages



They determine how components live together.



4\. Patterns — The Architectural Rhythms

Patterns are the reusable compositions:

\- hero sections

\- dashboard grids

\- multi-step flows

\- feature blocks

\- content structures



They give Ravlo its cinematic, blueprint-like identity.



5\. Guidelines — The Rules of the World

Guidelines define:

\- accessibility

\- responsiveness

\- motion

\- writing

\- theming



They ensure Ravlo is not just beautiful — but usable, inclusive, and scalable.



6\. Resources — The Toolkit

Assets, templates, downloads, and libraries that help teams build consistently.
