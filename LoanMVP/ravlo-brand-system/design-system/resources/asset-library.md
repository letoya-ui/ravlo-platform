Assets



The Assets library provides all downloadable files, brand materials, and system resources required to implement the Ravlo design system across product, marketing, and partner experiences.



Ravlo assets are:

\- organized

\- reusable

\- implementation‑ready

\- premium

\- consistent



1\. Purpose

Assets give designers and engineers direct access to the core building blocks of the Ravlo brand and product experience.



Assets include:

\- logos and monograms

\- brand imagery

\- icon sets

\- UI frames

\- blueprint overlays

\- marketing visuals

\- architectural presets

\- downloadable templates



2\. Asset Categories



Logos  

Primary logo, monogram, wordmark, and approved variations.



Brand Imagery  

Cinematic, architectural photography and hero visuals.



Icons  

Minimal, geometric icon library aligned with Ravlo’s stroke rules.



UI Frames  

Device frames, browser frames, and product mockup frames.



Blueprint Assets  

Grids, overlays, lines, and architectural blueprint elements.



Marketing Visuals  

Hero images, section backgrounds, and cinematic gradients.



Architectural Presets  

Style presets for Build Studio and Rehab Studio.



Templates  

Downloadable templates for presentations, marketing, and documentation.



3\. File Formats



Assets are provided in:

\- SVG (preferred for UI and logos)

\- PNG (transparent backgrounds)

\- JPG (photography and imagery)

\- WebP (optimized web assets)

\- PDF (print‑ready brand materials)



4\. Usage Guidelines



Assets must:

\- follow Ravlo’s brand rules

\- maintain original proportions

\- avoid unauthorized recoloring

\- use approved backgrounds

\- preserve clarity and cinematic tone



Do not:

\- distort or stretch assets

\- apply unapproved filters

\- place assets on busy or low‑contrast backgrounds

\- modify brand marks or monograms



5\. Organization



Assets are organized by:

\- category

\- format

\- resolution

\- usage context (product, marketing, partner)



Each asset includes:

\- name

\- description

\- usage notes

\- recommended contexts



6\. Updates



The asset library is updated as:

\- new components are added

\- new marketing visuals are created

\- brand refinements are introduced

\- new product modules launch



Assets must always reflect the latest version of the Ravlo brand system.



The Assets library is the practical toolkit of the Ravlo design system — everything teams need to build with clarity, consistency, and cinematic presence.
