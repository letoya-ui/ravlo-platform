Marketing Layouts



Marketing layouts define the structure of Ravlo’s public‑facing website, landing pages, and product storytelling surfaces. These layouts communicate Ravlo’s identity, philosophy, and value with clarity and cinematic presence.



Ravlo marketing layouts are designed to feel:

\- premium

\- cinematic

\- spacious

\- intentional

\- unified with the app



1\. Purpose

Marketing layouts introduce Ravlo to investors, partners, and builders. They communicate the brand’s philosophy, product value, and ecosystem in a way that feels confident and elevated.



Marketing layouts are used for:

\- homepage

\- product pages

\- partner pages

\- pricing

\- about

\- support and FAQ

\- landing pages

\- storytelling sections



2\. Core Marketing Structures

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of marketing layout patterns:



Hero Layout

A bold, cinematic introduction with a clear headline, subtext, and primary action.



Feature Grid Layout

A structured grid that highlights product features, modules, or capabilities.



Split Layout

A two‑column layout with text on one side and imagery or product visuals on the other.



Cinematic Section Layout

A wide, immersive layout used for storytelling, philosophy, or brand moments.



Card‑Based Marketing Layout

A modular layout using cards to highlight features, partners, or benefits.



Long‑Form Content Layout

A centered, readable layout for documentation, philosophy, or narrative content.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo marketing layout includes:

\- global spacing and grid alignment

\- hero section or page header

\- structured content blocks

\- optional imagery or product visuals

\- consistent typography and spacing tokens

\- responsive breakpoints for all screen sizes



4\. Behavior

Marketing layouts must:

\- maintain a premium, cinematic feel

\- avoid clutter or overwhelming visuals

\- guide the user through a clear narrative

\- highlight product value with intention

\- remain consistent with the Ravlo dashboard aesthetic

\- scale gracefully across devices



5\. Design Principles

Marketing layouts follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: clean, architectural structure

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room and visual rhythm

\- Clarity over cleverness: direct, confident storytelling

\- Emotional safety: calm, grounded presentation



Marketing layouts are the front door to the Ravlo ecosystem. They set the tone, communicate the brand, and invite users into a world built with discipline and intention.
