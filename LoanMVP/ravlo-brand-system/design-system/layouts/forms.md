Form Layouts



Form layouts define the structure of Ravlo’s data‑entry experiences. They support everything from quick inputs to multi‑step workflows, ensuring clarity, predictability, and investor‑grade flow.



Ravlo form layouts are designed to feel:

\- structured

\- calm

\- guided

\- predictable

\- cinematic



1\. Purpose

Form layouts organize fields, sections, and actions into a clear, intentional flow. They reduce friction, prevent overwhelm, and help users complete tasks with confidence.



Form layouts are used for:

\- deal analysis inputs

\- borrower and partner onboarding

\- renovation and build planning

\- capital requests

\- settings and preferences

\- document workflows

\- multi‑step applications



2\. Core Form Structures

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of form layout patterns:



Single‑Column Layout

A simple, focused layout for short or medium‑length forms.



Two‑Column Layout

A balanced layout for structured data entry, often used in investor workflows.



Sectioned Layout

A layout divided into labeled sections for clarity and organization.



Multi‑Step Layout

A guided, step‑based flow for complex tasks such as underwriting or onboarding.



Panel‑Based Layout

A layout where forms live inside panels for structure and hierarchy.



Review Layout

A layout that displays entered data for confirmation before submission.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo form layout includes:

\- clear section headers

\- grouped fields

\- consistent spacing and alignment

\- helper text and validation messages

\- primary and secondary actions

\- responsive breakpoints for all screen sizes



4\. Behavior

Form layouts must:

\- avoid overwhelming users with too many fields at once

\- maintain consistent spacing and rhythm

\- support keyboard navigation and accessibility

\- provide clear progress indicators in multi‑step flows

\- separate content into logical, scannable sections

\- reinforce Ravlo’s calm, cinematic presentation



5\. Design Principles

Form layouts follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: strict grid and structural clarity

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: simple, readable organization

\- Emotional safety: predictable, guided flow



Form layouts are the engine of data collection in the Ravlo OS. They turn complex workflows into clear, confident steps.
