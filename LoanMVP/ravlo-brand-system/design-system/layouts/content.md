Content Layouts



Content layouts define the structure of Ravlo’s long‑form, informational, and documentation‑based pages. These layouts support clarity, readability, and calm presentation for text‑heavy or knowledge‑driven content.



Ravlo content layouts are designed to feel:

\- readable

\- calm

\- structured

\- spacious

\- cinematic



1\. Purpose

Content layouts organize information in a way that supports learning, scanning, and deep understanding. They ensure that long‑form content never feels overwhelming or cluttered.



Content layouts are used for:

\- documentation

\- knowledge base articles

\- help and support pages

\- product explanations

\- long‑form storytelling

\- internal guides

\- legal and policy pages



2\. Core Content Structures

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of content layout patterns:



Centered Content Layout

A clean, centered column with generous spacing for long‑form reading.



Two‑Column Content Layout

A layout with a main reading column and a secondary column for navigation or metadata.



Sidebar Navigation Layout

A layout with persistent navigation on the left and content on the right.



Sectioned Content Layout

A layout divided into clear sections with headers and spacing for readability.



Cinematic Content Layout

A wide, immersive layout used for storytelling, philosophy, or brand narrative.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo content layout includes:

\- consistent typography and spacing tokens

\- clear section headers

\- readable line length

\- optional sidebar or table of contents

\- responsive breakpoints for all screen sizes

\- calm, predictable rhythm



4\. Behavior

Content layouts must:

\- prioritize readability above all else

\- maintain consistent spacing and hierarchy

\- avoid dense or overwhelming blocks of text

\- support scanning with clear headers and spacing

\- scale gracefully on mobile

\- reinforce Ravlo’s cinematic, architectural identity



5\. Design Principles

Content layouts follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: clean, architectural structure

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: simple, readable presentation

\- Emotional safety: calm, grounded content flow



Content layouts are the knowledge surfaces of the Ravlo OS. They turn information into clarity and help users learn, understand, and move forward with confidence.
