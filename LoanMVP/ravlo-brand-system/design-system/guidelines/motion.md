Motion



Motion in Ravlo is subtle, cinematic, and purposeful. It reinforces clarity, guides attention, and supports investor‑grade decision making without distraction or noise.



Ravlo motion is:

\- calm

\- intentional

\- minimal

\- cinematic

\- supportive

\- predictable



1\. Purpose

Motion provides feedback, communicates hierarchy, and creates a sense of depth and presence. It should never overwhelm, distract, or call attention to itself.



Motion defines:

\- transitions

\- animations

\- easing curves

\- timing

\- feedback behavior

\- motion accessibility



2\. Principles



Cinematic Subtlety  

Motion should feel soft, atmospheric, and grounded — never flashy.



Clarity First  

Motion must reinforce understanding, not decoration.



Predictability  

Animations should behave consistently across components and surfaces.



Emotional Safety  

Avoid sudden, rapid, or unexpected movement.



3\. Timing \& Easing



Timing  

\- Fast interactions: 120–160ms  

\- Medium transitions: 180–240ms  

\- Cinematic fades: 240–320ms  



Easing  

\- Use soft, natural easing curves  

\- Avoid sharp or bouncy easings  

\- Maintain consistency across all components  



4\. Usage



Use motion to:

\- guide attention  

\- confirm actions  

\- reveal hierarchy  

\- support navigation  

\- soften transitions  



Avoid motion that:

\- distracts from content  

\- feels playful or exaggerated  

\- introduces cognitive load  



5\. Component Motion



Cards  

\- soft elevation on hover  

\- subtle fade on load  



Modals  

\- fade + slight upward shift  

\- dimmed cinematic backdrop  



Panels  

\- slide‑in with soft easing  

\- fade on exit  



Buttons  

\- minimal hover lift  

\- subtle press feedback  



6\. Motion Accessibility



Rules:

\- provide reduced‑motion mode  

\- avoid flashing or rapid movement  

\- ensure motion is never required to understand content  

\- maintain clarity without animation  



Motion in Ravlo is a cinematic layer — subtle, intentional, and always in service of clarity.
