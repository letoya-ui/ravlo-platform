Theming



Theming defines how Ravlo’s visual identity adapts across modes, surfaces, and contexts while maintaining a unified architectural aesthetic.



Ravlo theming is:

\- consistent

\- scalable

\- disciplined

\- dark‑mode‑first

\- premium



1\. Purpose

Theming ensures that color, surfaces, and materials remain consistent across modules, patterns, and components — even as contexts change.



Theming defines:

\- dark mode

\- light mode (optional)

\- surface layers

\- semantic states

\- brand accents

\- contextual themes



2\. Dark Mode (Primary)



Dark mode is Ravlo’s core identity.



Characteristics:

\- deep, matte surfaces  

\- layered depth  

\- soft glows  

\- blueprint accents  

\- cinematic lighting  



3\. Light Mode (Optional)



Light mode is secondary and used sparingly.



Characteristics:

\- soft neutrals  

\- architectural whites  

\- controlled contrast  

\- minimal shadows  



4\. Surface System



Surfaces include:

\- base  

\- elevated  

\- panel  

\- card  

\- overlay  



Rules:

\- maintain consistent depth hierarchy  

\- avoid overly bright surfaces  

\- preserve calm, cinematic tone  



5\. Semantic States



States must:

\- remain accessible  

\- avoid saturation  

\- maintain clarity  

\- use color sparingly  



6\. Brand Accents



Accents:

\- blueprint blue  

\- steel neutrals  

\- soft glows  



Usage:

\- highlight key actions  

\- reinforce brand identity  

\- avoid overuse  



7\. Contextual Themes



Used for:

\- dashboards  

\- flows  

\- marketing  

\- partner tools  



Rules:

\- maintain core brand identity  

\- adapt only spacing, density, or emphasis  

\- never break architectural structure  



Theming ensures Ravlo remains unified, premium, and cinematic across all contexts.
