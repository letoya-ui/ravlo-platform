Guidelines



Guidelines define the rules, behaviors, and best practices that ensure Ravlo’s design system is applied consistently and intentionally across all surfaces. They translate Ravlo’s principles into actionable standards for designers, engineers, and contributors.



Ravlo’s guidelines are:

\- disciplined

\- architectural

\- predictable

\- premium

\- calm

\- intentional



1\. Purpose

Guidelines provide the operational rules that govern how components, patterns, and layouts should be used. They ensure clarity, consistency, and investor‑grade quality across the entire platform.



Guidelines define:

\- accessibility rules

\- content and writing standards

\- interaction behavior

\- spacing and layout rules

\- component usage rules

\- responsive behavior

\- do’s and don’ts



2\. Core Principles

All guidelines follow Ravlo’s foundational principles:



Blueprint Alignment  

Structure, precision, and disciplined grid usage.



Cinematic Spacing  

Generous breathing room that creates calm and clarity.



Clarity Over Cleverness  

Simple, intentional decisions that prioritize understanding.



Emotional Safety  

Predictable, grounded experiences that reduce cognitive load.



3\. Guideline Categories



Accessibility  

Rules for contrast, keyboard navigation, focus states, and inclusive design.



Content \& Writing  

Standards for clarity, tone, hierarchy, and readability.



Interaction  

Guidelines for hover, focus, active states, transitions, and feedback.



Layout \& Spacing  

Rules for grid usage, spacing tokens, alignment, and responsive behavior.



Component Usage  

Instructions for when and how to use each component correctly.



Do’s \& Don’ts  

Clear examples of correct and incorrect usage across the system.



4\. Behavior

Guidelines must:

\- remain consistent across modules and surfaces

\- support predictable, investor‑grade workflows

\- reduce ambiguity for designers and engineers

\- reinforce Ravlo’s premium, cinematic identity

\- scale across product, marketing, and partner tools



5\. Design Principles

Guidelines follow Ravlo’s core design principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: disciplined structure

\- Cinematic spacing: calm, intentional rhythm

\- Clarity over cleverness: direct, readable decisions

\- Emotional safety: grounded, predictable behavior



Guidelines are the operational backbone of the Ravlo design system. They ensure every decision — visual, structural, or interactive — aligns with Ravlo’s architectural identity.
