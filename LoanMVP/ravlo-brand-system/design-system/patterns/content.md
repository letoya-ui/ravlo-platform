Content Patterns



Content patterns define the reusable structures used to present long‑form information, documentation, knowledge, and narrative content across the Ravlo ecosystem. They ensure clarity, readability, and a calm, cinematic flow.



Ravlo content patterns are designed to feel:

\- readable

\- structured

\- calm

\- spacious

\- intentional



1\. Purpose

Content patterns organize information into predictable, digestible structures. They help users learn, understand, and navigate knowledge without feeling overwhelmed.



Content patterns are used for:

\- documentation

\- knowledge base articles

\- help and support content

\- product explanations

\- long‑form storytelling

\- internal guides

\- legal and policy pages



2\. Types of Content Patterns

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of content pattern structures:



Standard Content Block

A clean, centered block of text with clear hierarchy and spacing.



Sectioned Content Block

A pattern that divides content into labeled sections for readability.



Two‑Column Content Block

A layout with a main reading column and a secondary column for navigation or metadata.



Callout Block

A highlighted block used for important notes, warnings, or insights.



Cinematic Content Block

A wide, immersive block used for storytelling or brand narrative.



List + Detail Block

A pattern where a list on the left controls detailed content on the right.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo content pattern includes:

\- clear headers and subheaders

\- readable line length

\- spacing and rhythm based on tokens

\- optional sidebar or navigation

\- consistent typography

\- responsive behavior across breakpoints



4\. Behavior

Content patterns must:

\- prioritize readability above all else

\- maintain consistent spacing and hierarchy

\- avoid dense or overwhelming blocks of text

\- support scanning with clear headers and spacing

\- scale gracefully on mobile

\- reinforce Ravlo’s calm, cinematic identity



5\. Design Principles

Content patterns follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: disciplined structure and grid

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: simple, readable presentation

\- Emotional safety: calm, grounded content flow



Content patterns are the knowledge structures of the Ravlo OS. They transform information into clarity and help users move forward with confidence.
