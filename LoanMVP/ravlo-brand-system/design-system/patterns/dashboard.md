Dashboard Patterns



Dashboard patterns define the reusable structures that shape Ravlo’s investor‑grade command centers. They organize metrics, cards, panels, and insights into clear, cinematic layouts that support fast decision‑making.



Ravlo dashboard patterns are designed to feel:

\- data‑driven

\- architectural

\- modular

\- calm

\- investor‑grade



1\. Purpose

Dashboard patterns create predictable, reusable structures for presenting insights, activity, and analysis. They ensure dashboards across modules feel unified, intentional, and optimized for scanning.



Dashboard patterns are used for:

\- investor home

\- deal overview

\- portfolio intelligence

\- partner activity

\- build and renovation tracking

\- capital and financial insights



2\. Types of Dashboard Patterns

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of dashboard pattern structures:



Hero Metrics Row

A top row of key metrics that sets the tone for the dashboard.



Metric Grid

A structured grid of KPIs for quick scanning and comparison.



Card Grid

A modular grid of cards used for insights, activity, or summaries.



Panel + Cards Pattern

A layout with a primary panel on one side and supporting cards on the other.



Activity Stream Pattern

A vertically‑stacked feed of updates, events, or partner activity.



Snapshot Pattern

A compact overview of recent performance, tasks, or alerts.



Three‑Column Data Pattern

A dense, investor‑grade structure for data‑heavy dashboards.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo dashboard pattern includes:

\- clear hierarchy of metrics, cards, and panels

\- spacing and rhythm based on tokens

\- grid alignment for all content blocks

\- optional filters or quick actions

\- responsive behavior across breakpoints



4\. Behavior

Dashboard patterns must:

\- surface the most important information first

\- maintain consistent spacing and structure

\- avoid visual noise or overcrowding

\- support scanning and comparison

\- adapt gracefully to different screen sizes

\- reinforce Ravlo’s cinematic, architectural identity



5\. Design Principles

Dashboard patterns follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: disciplined grid and structure

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: simple, intentional presentation

\- Emotional safety: calm, predictable dashboards



Dashboard patterns are the analytical backbone of the Ravlo OS. They turn complex data into clarity, helping investors understand their world at a glance.
