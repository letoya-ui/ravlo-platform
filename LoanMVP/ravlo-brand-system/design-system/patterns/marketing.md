Marketing Patterns



Marketing patterns define the reusable structures used across Ravlo’s public‑facing website, landing pages, and product storytelling surfaces. They bring cinematic presence, clarity, and intentional rhythm to the Ravlo brand.



Ravlo marketing patterns are designed to feel:

\- premium

\- cinematic

\- spacious

\- intentional

\- unified with the app



1\. Purpose

Marketing patterns communicate Ravlo’s identity, philosophy, and product value. They create a consistent storytelling language that feels confident, elevated, and unmistakably Ravlo.



Marketing patterns are used for:

\- homepage sections

\- product storytelling

\- partner and ecosystem highlights

\- pricing and plan explanations

\- landing pages

\- brand philosophy sections

\- feature showcases



2\. Types of Marketing Patterns

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of marketing pattern structures:



Cinematic Hero Pattern

A bold, immersive hero with a headline, subtext, and primary action.



Split Story Pattern

A two‑column pattern with narrative text on one side and imagery or product visuals on the other.



Feature Grid Pattern

A structured grid highlighting product capabilities or ecosystem features.



Cinematic Section Pattern

A wide, spacious section used for storytelling, philosophy, or brand moments.



Card Marketing Pattern

A modular grid of cards used to showcase features, partners, or benefits.



Metric Story Pattern

A pattern that uses numbers and impact metrics to communicate value.



Process Story Pattern

A step‑based pattern that explains how Ravlo works or how a workflow unfolds.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo marketing pattern includes:

\- clear headline or narrative text

\- supporting description

\- optional imagery or product visuals

\- spacing and rhythm based on tokens

\- grid alignment for all content

\- responsive behavior across breakpoints



4\. Behavior

Marketing patterns must:

\- maintain a premium, cinematic feel

\- avoid clutter or overwhelming visuals

\- guide the user through a clear narrative

\- highlight product value with intention

\- remain consistent with the Ravlo dashboard aesthetic

\- scale gracefully across devices



5\. Design Principles

Marketing patterns follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: clean, architectural structure

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: direct, confident storytelling

\- Emotional safety: calm, grounded presentation



Marketing patterns are the storytelling DNA of Ravlo. They communicate value, philosophy, and identity with clarity and cinematic presence.
