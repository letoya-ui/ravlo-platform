Patterns



Patterns are high‑level, reusable structures that define the visual rhythm and architectural flow of the Ravlo interface. They sit above components and layouts, providing the blueprint for how content, features, and workflows are presented across the Ravlo ecosystem.



Ravlo patterns are designed to feel:

\- cinematic

\- architectural

\- modular

\- consistent

\- unmistakably Ravlo



1\. Purpose

Patterns create predictable, reusable structures that ensure every page, module, and workflow feels unified. They reduce design drift, speed up development, and maintain Ravlo’s premium, blueprint‑inspired identity.



Patterns are used for:

\- dashboards

\- hero sections

\- flows and multi‑step processes

\- feature highlights

\- content pages

\- marketing surfaces

\- investor workflows



2\. Pattern Categories

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of pattern categories:



Hero Patterns

Large, cinematic structures used for introductions, overviews, and high‑impact moments.



Dashboard Patterns

Grid‑based structures for metrics, cards, and investor insights.



Flow Patterns

Step‑based structures for guided workflows and processes.



Feature Block Patterns

Modular blocks used for highlighting features, benefits, or product capabilities.



Content Patterns

Structured layouts for long‑form reading, documentation, and knowledge surfaces.



Marketing Patterns

Cinematic, storytelling‑driven structures for ravlohq.com and product pages.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo pattern includes:

\- a defined structure or grid

\- spacing and rhythm based on tokens

\- clear hierarchy and alignment

\- reusable content regions

\- optional imagery or iconography

\- responsive behavior across breakpoints



4\. Behavior

Patterns must:

\- remain consistent across modules and surfaces

\- support flexible content without breaking structure

\- maintain Ravlo’s cinematic spacing

\- avoid clutter or visual noise

\- reinforce investor‑grade clarity and confidence



5\. Design Principles

Patterns follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: disciplined structure and grid

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: simple, intentional composition

\- Emotional safety: calm, grounded presentation



Patterns are the architectural DNA of the Ravlo OS. They ensure every experience feels unified, premium, and unmistakably Ravlo.
