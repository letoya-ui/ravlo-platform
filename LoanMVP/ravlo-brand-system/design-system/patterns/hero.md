Hero Patterns



Hero patterns are large, cinematic structures used to introduce a page, module, or workflow. They set the tone, establish hierarchy, and create a strong first impression that feels unmistakably Ravlo.



Ravlo hero patterns are designed to feel:

\- bold

\- architectural

\- cinematic

\- intentional

\- premium



1\. Purpose

Hero patterns create clarity and presence at the top of a page. They orient the user, communicate the purpose of the screen, and establish the visual rhythm for everything that follows.



Hero patterns are used for:

\- dashboards

\- OS modules

\- marketing pages

\- content intros

\- workflow beginnings

\- investor overviews



2\. Types of Hero Patterns

Ravlo uses a disciplined set of hero pattern structures:



Standard Hero

A clean, centered hero with a title, subtitle, and optional actions.



Split Hero

A two‑column hero with text on one side and imagery or data on the other.



Centered Hero

A minimal, centered layout used for calm, focused introductions.



Metric Hero

A hero that includes key metrics or insights at the top of a dashboard.



Cinematic Hero

A wide, immersive hero used for storytelling or brand moments.



3\. Anatomy

A Ravlo hero pattern includes:

\- page title or section title

\- optional subtitle or supporting text

\- optional primary and secondary actions

\- spacing based on tokens

\- alignment based on grid rules

\- optional imagery, metrics, or iconography



4\. Behavior

Hero patterns must:

\- establish clear hierarchy

\- avoid clutter or unnecessary decoration

\- maintain generous spacing

\- support responsive resizing

\- reinforce the tone of the page or module

\- remain consistent across surfaces



5\. Design Principles

Hero patterns follow Ravlo’s core principles:

\- Blueprint alignment: clean, architectural structure

\- Cinematic spacing: generous breathing room

\- Clarity over cleverness: simple, intentional composition

\- Emotional safety: calm, grounded presentation



Hero patterns are the opening statement of the Ravlo OS. They set the tone, establish presence, and guide users into the experience with clarity and confidence.
