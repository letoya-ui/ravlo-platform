Components



Components are the reusable building blocks of the Ravlo interface. They are the structural elements that appear across dashboards, workflows, OS modules, and marketing surfaces.



Each component is designed to be:

\- consistent

\- modular

\- token-driven

\- accessible

\- scalable



Components form the foundation of Ravlo’s visual language. They ensure that every interaction feels familiar, predictable, and unmistakably Ravlo.



Components include:

\- buttons

\- cards

\- panels

\- metrics

\- navigation

\- tables

\- modals

\- forms

\- badges

\- inputs



Every component follows the same rules:

1\. Use design tokens for spacing, color, typography, radii, and shadows.

2\. Maintain clear hierarchy and alignment.

3\. Support responsive behavior.

4\. Prioritize clarity over decoration.

5\. Reinforce Ravlo’s cinematic, blueprint-inspired identity.



Components are the pieces that make Ravlo feel unified, stable, and investor‑grade.
