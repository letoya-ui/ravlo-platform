Logo



The Ravlo logo represents the core identity of the platform — architectural precision, cinematic presence, and disciplined minimalism. It must always appear intentional, premium, and unmistakably Ravlo.



Ravlo’s logo system is:

\- architectural

\- geometric

\- minimal

\- premium

\- confident



1\. Purpose

The logo is the primary visual mark of the Ravlo brand. It communicates trust, structure, and clarity across product, marketing, and partner experiences.



The logo system defines:

\- primary logo

\- monogram

\- wordmark

\- spacing rules

\- sizing rules

\- usage guidelines

\- restrictions



2\. Logo Components



Primary Logo  

The full Ravlo logo combining the monogram and wordmark. Used for major brand moments, marketing, and high‑visibility surfaces.



Monogram (RV)  

A geometric, architectural symbol used for compact spaces such as:

\- app icons

\- avatars

\- buttons

\- badges

\- loading states

\- login hero



Wordmark  

The “RAVLO” text set in the brand typeface with disciplined spacing and strong geometry. Used in headers, footers, and product surfaces.



3\. Clear Space

The logo must always have generous breathing room.



Clear space rules:

\- minimum padding equal to the height of the “R”

\- no elements may intrude into this space

\- maintain extra spacing on cinematic surfaces



4\. Sizing

The logo must remain legible and balanced at all sizes.



Minimum sizes:

\- monogram: 24px

\- wordmark: 48px

\- full logo: 64px



Larger sizes should maintain proportional scaling.



5\. Color Usage

The logo uses Ravlo’s premium, dark‑mode‑first palette.



Approved colors:

\- white (primary)

\- blueprint blue (accent)

\- steel (subtle surfaces)

\- gradient glow (cinematic hero moments)



Avoid:

\- bright colors

\- saturated tones

\- gradients not defined in the brand system



6\. Backgrounds

The logo must appear on calm, controlled backgrounds.



Approved backgrounds:

\- dark surfaces

\- slate

\- steel

\- muted gradients

\- architectural imagery with low contrast



Avoid:

\- busy textures

\- bright or saturated backgrounds

\- low‑contrast placements



7\. Usage Guidelines

The logo must:

\- never be stretched or distorted

\- never be rotated

\- never be placed inside shapes unless defined by the system

\- never use shadows outside approved cinematic glow

\- never be recolored outside the brand palette



8\. Monogram Usage

The RV monogram is used for:

\- app icon

\- login hero

\- dashboard avatar

\- partner badges

\- compact UI elements



The monogram must always appear centered, balanced, and treated as a premium architectural symbol.



9\. Wordmark Usage

The wordmark is used for:

\- headers

\- footers

\- product surfaces

\- marketing sections

\- documentation branding



The wordmark must always maintain its original spacing and geometry.



The Ravlo logo is the anchor of the brand. It communicates structure, clarity, and cinematic presence — the foundation of the Ravlo identity.
