Typography



Ravlo’s typography system defines the voice, rhythm, and structure of all written communication across the platform. It creates clarity, hierarchy, and a premium, architectural presence that aligns with Ravlo’s cinematic identity.



Ravlo typography is:

\- clean

\- modern

\- architectural

\- disciplined

\- calm

\- highly readable



1\. Purpose

The typography system ensures consistency and clarity across product, marketing, and documentation. It establishes a predictable hierarchy that supports investor‑grade decision making and calm, confident reading.



The typography system defines:

\- typefaces

\- hierarchy and scale

\- line height and spacing

\- weight usage

\- alignment rules

\- readability guidelines



2\. Typeface

Ravlo uses a modern, geometric sans‑serif typeface that reflects architectural precision and cinematic clarity.



Typeface characteristics:

\- clean geometry

\- balanced proportions

\- strong vertical rhythm

\- excellent readability at all sizes



3\. Hierarchy \& Scale

Ravlo typography uses a disciplined hierarchy to create structure and clarity.



Hierarchy levels include:

\- Display headings (cinematic, used sparingly)

\- Page titles

\- Section headers

\- Subheaders

\- Body text (primary)

\- Secondary text (supporting)

\- Caption and metadata text



Rules:

\- hierarchy must be clear and consistent

\- spacing between levels must follow tokens

\- headings should not be overused



4\. Line Height \& Spacing

Typography spacing reinforces Ravlo’s calm, cinematic rhythm.



Rules:

\- generous line height for readability

\- consistent spacing between text blocks

\- predictable vertical rhythm across surfaces

\- controlled line length for long‑form content



5\. Weight Usage

Weights are used intentionally to maintain clarity and hierarchy.



Approved weights:

\- Regular (default)

\- Medium (subtle emphasis)

\- Semibold (headers and key labels)

\- Bold (rare, used only for strong emphasis)



Avoid:

\- excessive bolding

\- mixing too many weights in one block



6\. Alignment

Alignment reinforces structure and architectural clarity.



Rules:

\- left alignment for most content

\- centered alignment for cinematic or hero moments

\- avoid justified text

\- maintain consistent grid alignment



7\. Readability Guidelines

Typography must:

\- support scanning and comprehension

\- avoid dense or overwhelming blocks

\- maintain accessible contrast

\- use clear hierarchy for long‑form content

\- pair well with Ravlo’s dark, cinematic palette



8\. Tone Reinforcement

Typography expresses Ravlo’s brand tone:

\- confident, not loud

\- calm, not passive

\- structured, not rigid

\- premium, not flashy



Typography is the voice of Ravlo’s visual identity. It brings clarity, structure, and cinematic presence to every surface.
