\# Social Assets



This folder contains all social media visuals for Ravlo.



Platforms:

\- Instagram

\- LinkedIn

\- Twitter/X

\- YouTube thumbnails

\- Facebook groups

\- TikTok covers



Use for:

\- Product announcements

\- Feature highlights

\- OS visuals

\- Brand storytelling



Naming Convention:

ravlo-social-\[platform]-\[topic]-v1.\[format]



Notes:

\- Maintain cinematic OS style.

\- Use tall frames for TikTok/Reels.

\- Use wide frames for YouTube and LinkedIn.
