\# Press Assets



This folder contains assets used for press kits, media coverage, and official communications.



Includes:

\- Brand logos

\- Wordmark variations

\- Monogram assets

\- Hero OS visuals

\- Ecosystem diagram

\- Product module visuals



Naming Convention:

ravlo-press-\[asset]-v1.\[format]



Notes:

\- Keep assets clean and high resolution.

\- Use straight-on UI frames for clarity.

\- Avoid heavy glow in press materials.
