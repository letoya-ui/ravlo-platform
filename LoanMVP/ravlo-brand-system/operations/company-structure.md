# Caughman Mason LLC and Ravlo Company Structure

## Parent company

Caughman Mason LLC is the parent business entity.

Ravlo operates under Caughman Mason LLC.

This structure allows the company to keep the family name while building distinct operating brands and divisions inside the business.

---

## Brand architecture

### Caughman Mason LLC

Role: Parent company / ownership entity

Purpose:

- owns and supports the business ecosystem
- holds the family name
- can support multiple divisions, products, and operating lanes
- provides the legal and business umbrella for Ravlo and related services

### Ravlo

Role: Technology platform and operating system brand

Purpose:

- AI-powered real estate operating system
- Investor OS
- Lending OS
- Partner Network
- Academy
- Executive dashboards
- Business operations tools

Ravlo is the platform brand users recognize, subscribe to, and operate inside.

### Caughman Mason Construction

Role: Construction-side operating lane / construction partner division

Purpose:

- construction command center
- project execution
- rehab scopes
- renovation budgets
- contractor coordination
- Build Studio support
- field operations
- investor project support

Caughman Mason Construction can remain branded around the family name while still being connected to Ravlo's platform and partner ecosystem.

---

## Dashboard naming principle

Do not force every division to become Ravlo-branded.

Instead, dashboards should show the relationship clearly:

- Ravlo is the operating system.
- Caughman Mason LLC is the parent company.
- Caughman Mason Construction is the construction-side operating lane.

Recommended dashboard language:

- Page title: Construction Command Center
- Subline: Caughman Mason Construction · Powered by Ravlo
- Context: Build-side leadership for Caughman Mason LLC and Ravlo's real estate ecosystem

This respects the family name while making it clear that Ravlo is the system powering the work.

## Jamaine's role

Jamaine does not need to rename Caughman Mason Construction.

Recommended framing:

- Human title: Construction Partner / Construction Operations Lead
- Workspace: Construction Command Center
- Brand relationship: Caughman Mason Construction, powered by Ravlo
- Academy path: Contractor / Construction

Jamaine's dashboard should make him feel responsible for the construction side of the ecosystem without making him feel like he has to give up the Caughman Mason name.

## Letoya's role

Letoya leads the broader platform and company vision.

Recommended framing:

- Human title: Founder / CEO
- Workspace: Executive Mission Control
- Brand relationship: Ravlo under Caughman Mason LLC

Letoya's dashboard should remain the full Ravlo executive view across platform growth, users, subscriptions, lending, partners, Academy, and operations.

---

## Sandra's role

Sandra supports internal operations.

Recommended framing:

- Human title: Ravlo Operations Lead
- Workspace: Operations Center
- Academy path: Lending
- Company/team: Ravlo / Caughman Mason LLC internal team

Sandra helps operate Ravlo day to day while Letoya remains the final decision maker.

---

## Strategic note

This structure gives the business room to grow:

- Caughman Mason LLC remains the parent company.
- Ravlo remains the scalable technology brand.
- Caughman Mason Construction can remain the family construction lane.
- Future divisions can be added without confusing the public brand.

The goal is not to erase the family name. The goal is to make the relationship clear.
