# Team Academy Paths

Ravlo team members should have learning paths that match their responsibilities.

## Operations Lead

Primary team member: Sandra

Recommended Academy path: Lending

Secondary learning path: Bid Support / Construction Office Operations

Sandra should understand lending operations and the office side of construction bidding so she can support Jamaine without taking over his field responsibility.

### Sandra focus areas

- loan process
- borrower documentation
- lending operations
- CRM follow-up
- onboarding support
- processor and underwriting workflow awareness
- bid packet organization
- proposal formatting
- contractor intake notes
- project request follow-up
- site visit scheduling
- bid status tracking
- invoice and document follow-up

### Sandra bid-support expectations

Sandra can help with the office side of bids.

She can:

- receive new bid opportunities
- organize job details
- collect property address, contact info, photos, and documents
- schedule site visits
- prepare draft bid packets
- send follow-up emails
- update the CRM and construction pipeline
- track whether a bid is pending, sent, awarded, declined, or needs follow-up
- remind Jamaine what needs his field review

Sandra should not independently decide:

- final scope
- final labor number
- final material number
- final bid price
- whether Caughman Mason Construction accepts a project
- whether a construction job is safe, profitable, or properly licensed

Final field judgment stays with Jamaine.

Final business approval stays with Letoya when needed.

### Sandra daily bid-support routine

1. Check new requests and messages.
2. Confirm whether any opportunity needs a site visit.
3. Prepare job details for Jamaine before he goes to the field.
4. Update bid status after Jamaine reviews the job.
5. Send follow-up messages when a bid is waiting on a client, investor, or partner.
6. Add notes to the CRM/construction pipeline.
7. Include bid activity in the end-of-day report to Letoya.

### Sandra weekly bid-support goals

- keep every open construction opportunity updated
- make sure no bid sits without follow-up
- help Jamaine send or follow up on active bids
- flag missing information before it becomes an excuse
- keep Letoya informed on what is moving and what is stuck

## Construction Partner

Primary team member: Jamaine

Recommended Academy path: Contractor

Jamaine is the hands-on construction lead. He should not be expected to operate like an office manager, but he must be accountable for field execution and bid decisions.

### Jamaine focus areas

- rehab scopes
- repair estimates
- renovation budgets
- Build Studio workflow
- project timelines
- contractor coordination
- investor project support
- demo jobs
- small GC jobs
- site walks
- field photos and notes
- final bid review

### Jamaine expectations

Jamaine should focus on hands-on work, field relationships, and construction execution.

He is responsible for:

- reviewing job opportunities
- walking sites when needed
- taking field photos and notes
- giving Sandra the details needed to prepare or update bid packets
- confirming labor, material, and timeline assumptions
- approving final bid numbers before anything is sent as final
- identifying demo jobs, repair jobs, small GC jobs, and contractor relationships
- keeping the Construction Command Center updated enough that Letoya and Sandra can support him

The goal is not to make Jamaine sit at a desk all day.

The goal is to remove excuses by giving him a simple field-to-office system:

Field work by Jamaine.

Office organization by Sandra.

Final oversight by Letoya.

## Construction pipeline stages

Every construction opportunity should move through a simple status pipeline:

1. Opportunity
2. Site Visit Needed
3. Site Visit Scheduled
4. Estimate Needed
5. Draft Bid Prepared
6. Jamaine Review Needed
7. Bid Sent
8. Follow-Up Needed
9. Negotiating
10. Awarded
11. In Progress
12. Completed
13. Invoice Sent
14. Paid
15. Declined / Lost

This pipeline keeps the work objective. Nobody has to guess what is happening with a job.

## Dashboard expectation

The Operations Center should link the Operations Lead to the Lending Academy path and include bid-support expectations.

The Partner Network or Construction Command Center should link the Construction Partner to the Contractor Academy path.

Jamaine's dashboard should stay field-focused.

Sandra's dashboard should stay office-support focused.

Letoya's dashboard should show what is stuck, what is moving, and what needs executive attention.
