# Ravlo Color System
The cinematic palette that defines the Ravlo Visual OS ó midnight tones, blueprint accents, and premium investor-grade neutrals.

---

# 1. Core Palette

These colors form the foundation of the Ravlo universe.

### Midnight Black
- **Hex:** #0A0D12  
- **Usage:** Primary background, atmosphere base  
- **Mood:** Deep, calm, cinematic  

### Deep Navy
- **Hex:** #0F1621  
- **Usage:** Panels, sections, hero surfaces  
- **Mood:** Architectural, premium  

### Steel Blue
- **Hex:** #1C2A3A  
- **Usage:** Cards, panel internals, UI surfaces  
- **Mood:** Technical, structured  

### Blueprint Cyan
- **Hex:** #4CC3FF  
- **Usage:** Glows, blueprint rings, accents  
- **Mood:** Precision, clarity, engineered  

### Soft White
- **Hex:** #F2F5F8  
- **Usage:** Typography, highlights  
- **Mood:** Clean, readable, calm

# 4. Surface Layers

Ravlo uses layered surfaces to create depth.

### Base Surface
- Midnight Black (#0A0D12)  
- Used for full-page backgrounds  

### Elevated Surface
- Deep Navy (#0F1621)  
- Used for sections and panels  

### Panel Surface
- Steel Blue (#1C2A3A)  
- Used for floating panels  

### Card Surface
- Slightly lighter Steel Blue  
- Used for cards and small UI blocks  

### Overlay Surface
- Black with 40ñ60% opacity  
- Used for modals and cinematic overlays  

---

# 5. Gradients & Glows

Ravlo uses subtle, cinematic gradients.

### Blueprint Glow
- Blueprint Cyan ? Transparent  
- Soft, radial, low opacity  

### Midnight Gradient
- Deep Navy ? Midnight Black  
- Used for hero sections  

### Panel Glow
- Soft White or Blueprint Cyan  
- 4ñ8px blur  

### Rules
- Gradients must be subtle  
- Glows must guide attention  
- Never overpower content  

---

# 6. Color Usage Rules

1. Maintain contrast and accessibility  
2. Avoid bright or saturated tones  
3. Use accents sparingly  
4. Keep the palette calm and investor-grade  
5. Reinforce hierarchy and clarity  
6. Maintain consistency across modules  

---

# 7. Ravlo Color Principles

- Cinematic, not flashy  
- Architectural, not decorative  
- Calm, not chaotic  
- Premium, not playful  
- Technical, not sterile
