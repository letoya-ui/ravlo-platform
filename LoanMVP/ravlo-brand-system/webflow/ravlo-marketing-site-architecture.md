# Ravlo Marketing Site Architecture
The cinematic, investor-grade structure of the Ravlo public website — built to introduce the OS, modules, and ecosystem.

---

# 1. Marketing Site Philosophy

The Ravlo marketing site is:
- Cinematic and architectural  
- Calm and premium  
- Focused on clarity and trust  
- Designed to feel like the surface layer of the Ravlo OS  
- Structured to guide investors into the platform  

Every page should feel like a controlled, intentional reveal.

# 6. Content Guidelines

### Tone
- Confident  
- Clear  
- Investor-focused  
- Calm and cinematic

- Short sentences  
- Strong verbs  
- Minimal jargon  
- Clear value  

---

# 7. Motion Guidelines

- Fade-in + slide-up  
- Soft blueprint glow  
- Slow drift in hero  
- No aggressive parallax  
- Calm transitions  

---

# 8. Marketing Principles

1. Clarity over hype  
2. Calm over noise  
3. Architecture over decoration  
4. Consistency builds trust  
5. Every page introduces the OS
