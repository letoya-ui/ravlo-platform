# Ravlo Layout Patterns (High-Level Templates)
The macro-level structural templates that define the Ravlo OS experience across marketing, dashboard, and workflow surfaces.

---

# 1. Layout Philosophy

Ravlo layouts are:
- Cinematic and architectural  
- Calm and intentional  
- Built around clarity and hierarchy  
- Designed to guide decision-making  
- Consistent across all surfaces  

Layouts are the scaffolding of the OS.
