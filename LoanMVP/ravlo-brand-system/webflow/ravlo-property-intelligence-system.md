# Ravlo Property Intelligence System
The unified framework for property evaluation, risk analysis, renovation intelligence, and market context across the Ravlo OS.

---

# 1. Property Intelligence Philosophy

Ravlo property intelligence is:
- Calm  
- Clear  
- Data-backed  
- Explainable  
- Designed to reduce investor uncertainty  

Every insight should feel like a premium advisor — not a spreadsheet.

---
