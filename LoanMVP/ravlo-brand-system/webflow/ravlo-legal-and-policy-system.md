# Ravlo Legal & Policy System
The unified framework for legal, compliance, policy, and governance across the Ravlo OS.

---

# 1. Legal Philosophy

Ravlo legal is:
- Clear  
- Transparent  
- Protective  
- Compliant  
- Designed for long-term trust  

Policies should feel structured and predictable — never confusing.
