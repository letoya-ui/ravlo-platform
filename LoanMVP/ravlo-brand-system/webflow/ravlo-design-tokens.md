# Ravlo Design Tokens (Master Token Architecture)
The unified, platform-agnostic token system that powers the entire Ravlo OS ó colors, spacing, typography, motion, radii, shadows, grids, and states.

---

# 1. Token Philosophy

Ravlo tokens are:
- Atomic  
- Predictable  
- Scalable  
- Platform-agnostic  
- The single source of truth  

Tokens ensure Ravlo looks and feels consistent everywhere ó Webflow, React, mobile, and beyond.
