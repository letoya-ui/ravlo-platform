# Ravlo Growth & Experimentation System
The unified framework for experiments, analytics, optimization, and product evolution across the Ravlo OS.

---

# 1. Growth Philosophy

Ravlo growth is:
- Intentional  
- Measured  
- Calm  
- Data-driven  
- Aligned with the OS identity  

Growth should never create chaos — it should reinforce clarity.

---

# 5. Analytics System

### Data Sources
- App events  
- Workflow events  
- AI interactions  
- Marketplace actions  

### Requirements
- No PII in analytics  
- No sensitive data  
- Tokenized identifiers  

---

# 6. Experimentation Rules

### Rules
- Only one major experiment per workflow  
- Experiments must be reversible  
- Experiments must be documented  
- Experiments must not break accessibility  

### Duration
- Minimum: 7 days  
- Maximum: 30 days  

---
