# Ravlo Investor Intelligence System
The unified framework for property intelligence, portfolio insights, market analysis, and investor decision support across the Ravlo OS.

---

# 1. Intelligence Philosophy

Ravlo intelligence is:
- Calm  
- Clear  
- Actionable  
- Context-aware  
- Designed to reduce uncertainty  

Insights should feel like a trusted advisor — not a data dump.
